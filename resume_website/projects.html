≫Capstone 2017
A customized video player with the following features:
-Play/pause video on command, and otherwise act as a normal video player
-Pause the video, and play a second video
-During the second video, skipping/trackbar will be disabled
-Return to the first video, and restore the trackbar

The idea behind this app is that it would act as a player which would play advertisements, but with the ads hosted on site, so as not to be picked up by ad blocking software.
